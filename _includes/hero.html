Fear not, young grad student.

We remember our academic heroes for the best papers they wrote. But success means taking risks, and not every risk pans out. This page lists not just researchers' most highly cited paper from graduate school, but also their grad school papers that fell below the 50th percentile of citations for the venue that year. Let's celebrate those risks they took along the way.
