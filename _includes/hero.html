An ode to the struggles along the way.

Even our academic heroes met with lukewarm responses. This page contains papers that today's successful researchers published before finishing their PhDs — highlighting only the papers that fell below the 50th percentile of citations for the venue that year. Success comes with risktaking, so let's celebrate the risks.

To add someone to the list, add them to this spreadsheet. We crowdsourced the intial set on Daemo. Thanks to Semantic Scholar for the paper and citation dataset.
