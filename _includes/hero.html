The academic hero's path

I think of my academic heroes for the best papers they wrote. I judge myself for not living up to those standards. But those heroes didn't just Those papers were risks. Talking to these heroes, they tell me that it required risk-taking, and that not every risk they took paid off. This page lists well-known researchers — not just their most highly cited paper from graduate school, but also their grad school papers that fell below the 50th percentile of citations for the venue that year.
