An ode to the struggles.

Even our academic heroes met with lukewarm responses. This page contains papers that today's successful researchers published before finishing their PhDs — highlighting the papers that fell below the 50th percentile of citations for the venue that year. Success comes with risktaking, so let's celebrate the risks.
